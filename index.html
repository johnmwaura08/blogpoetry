Glimpses of my poetry

As the wind blows, you’ll find your node.
When the light glows, please take the road.
If the river flows, you’ll crack the code.
If at night you doze, you’ll leave me cold.

Literature

The grass on the sidewalk seems to lighten up as she walks by…
The dry humid earth gets moist when her shadow appears…
The ground seemed full with intent of rolling up and following…
A glimpse of her is enough to give full sight to a half-blind man…
She stands out in the green dress…
